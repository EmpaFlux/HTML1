About me

I am currently studying .Net development on my first year here in Gothenburg at IT-Högskolan, with the end goal of having the skills to be a full stack web developer upon graduation. The skills that we learn is primarily programing in C#, handling databases (Microsoft SQL, MongoDB, Azure Cosmos) and working with front end development (HTML, CSS and soon JavaScript).

I also have previously studied Gamewriting at the University of Skövde, and is enrolled in a game development studio that we started during school there, and we are currently in pre-production for our second title. The skills I gained there and are currently using in our company is primarily focused on narrative design, experimental storytelling, content writing (dialog, in-game text, barks etc) and character/world design.

Our Studio

We started out as a group of 25 students that together worked on Bleeper during a course in Skövde. Most of us felt that the game had potential and decided that we wanted to make a effort to complete it as a private venture, outside the school structure. Over the course of development the team was narrowed down to a core of four people (myself included), with many of our previous participants working for us a s freelancers. We finished our demo and went to a couple of expos with it and managed to score the best in show from Indie Games+ at Sweden Game Conference in 2018. Currently, we have our second game in pre-production and are looking towards a retail release as early as winter 2020.
